Frequently Asked Questions

Is JustHex really free? ▼
Yes! 100% free, forever. No hidden charges, no premium tiers, no login required. All tools are completely free to use.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to our servers. Your privacy is completely protected.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No signup required. You can start using tools immediately. Just visit, choose a tool, and go!
What tools do you offer? ▼
We offer 10 essential tools: Base64 Encoder, SHA256 Hash, MD5 Hash, JSON Formatter, URL Encoder, Password Generator, UUID Generator, Binary Converter, Case Converter, and Timestamp Converter.
Can I use these tools offline? ▼
Yes! Since all processing happens in your browser, you can save the pages and use them offline (though you'll need internet to first access the site).
How are you making money? ▼
JustHex is supported by non-intrusive advertising. We're committed to keeping the tools free while maintaining the service.
