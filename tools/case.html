Essential for coding, formatting content, adjusting naming conventions, and preparing text for different programming standards. Supports all text lengths and special characters.

💡 Case Format Guide
  • UPPERCASE: ALL WORDS IN CAPITALS
  • lowercase: all words in small letters
  • Title Case: First Letter Of Each Word Capitalized
  • camelCase: firstWordSmall, secondWordCapitalized
  • PascalCase: EachWordCapitalizedIncludingFirst
  • snake_case: words_separated_by_underscores
  • kebab-case: words-separated-by-hyphens
