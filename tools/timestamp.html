💡 Timestamp Quick Facts
  • UNIX Epoch starts at January 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC
  • Seconds timestamp: Represents single second precision
  • Milliseconds timestamp: 1000x larger, used in JavaScript
  • Current UNIX time increases by 1 every second
  • Used in databases, logs, APIs, and system timestamps
  • Timezone independent - always represents UTC
